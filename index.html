Features

All about the features

Revealing Elements on scroll

The webpage loads the content of the next page only on scrolling down. Refresh after moving to the top of the page and scroll down manually to see the effect.

Lazy loading of images

Watch the images load only it comes into the active screen. This saves data as a low resolution image is loaded initially during the start of the page.

Freezing the menu

The menu bar is frozen once the page is scrolled down to the next section.

Tabbed Contents

Everything as simple as possible, under a tab

01. Tab content selection

The contents are modified as per the corresponding tab. Select the other tabs to view their contents.

02. Tab display animation

The tabs are moved up as per the selection making it easier to identify the tab which is selected.

03. Blurry buttons

The active button in the header is highlighted by blurring the other buttons. Just hover over the buttons (Feature, Tabbed, Associates and Open Account) using your mouse and see the buttons other than the active button getting faded out.

Slider component

Millions of information under a slider component.

Explore the Slider component
Use the next --> and the previous <-- buttons to navigate through the components. Also see the dots in the bottom which highlights the active component and can also be used for navigating.
